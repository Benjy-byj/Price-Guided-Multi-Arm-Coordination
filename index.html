Price-Guided Multi-Arm Coordination

A PyBullet-based research prototype for space-time congestion pricing in shared multi-arm manipulation. The page opens directly on the recorded demo and starts playback automatically when the browser allows muted autoplay.

Scenario Four KUKA iiwa arms share a common workspace and coordinate through price signals on a space-time grid.
Modes The codebase includes static planning, online streaming targets, and batch-at-t=0 throughput evaluation.
Reproducibility Fixed seeds make target generation repeatable for batch and stream runs.
